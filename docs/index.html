Activity

GPS, Map, Activities (distance, pace, time)

Whether you’re training for a fun run or marathon, love running arround the city, a weekend warrior, turn your smartphone into a sophisticated running tracker with Takbo, break your personal record and reach your fitness goals!

With its GPS it can track stats like distance, pace and time, It has activity pause and resume function, GPS signal strength monitoring, updated map with current route that works on backgound mode to save your battery.

Privacy

Your device is the only one who have access to your activity records.

Takbo did not collect any personal data from its user, no need to login or input your height, weight, and age.

All activity records (distance, pace, time) are saved locally on your phone. As well as locations, time, and routes and will be deleted if the app is uninstalled.
