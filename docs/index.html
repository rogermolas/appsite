Activity

GPS, Map, Activities (distance, pace, time)

Whether you’re training for a fun run or marathon, love running arround the city, a weekend warrior, turn your smartphone into a sophisticated running tracker with Takbo, break your personal record and reach your fitness goals!

With its GPS it can track stats like distance, pace and time, It has activity pause and resume function, GPS signal strength monitoring, updated map with current route that works on backgound mode to save your battery.

Privacy

Your device is the only one who have access to your activity records.

Takbo did not collect any personal data from its user, no need to login or input your height, weight, and age.

All activity records (distance, pace, time) are saved locally on your phone. As well as locations, time, and routes and will be deleted if the app is uninstalled.

FOR EVENT ORGANIZER

SUBMIT YOUR EVENTS FOR FREE

  • 1
    Send us Email

    You can send us email at contact.takboapp@gmail.com, with your NAME, EVENT ORGANIZER, EVENT NAME, EVENT DATE and CONTACT NUMBER, with email subject EVENT SUBMISSION

  • 2
    Event Verification

    All submitted events will be verified before posting, you will recieve a call/email from our representative about your event submission status.

    REASON FOR REJECTION:

    Event duplicate, if the current event is already posted, organizer should send us update request via email.
    Event is submitted less than two weeks before the event date.
  • 3
    Event Posting

    Once the event pass the verification process you will recieve an email with link to our Event Posting Page

    Organizer should complete all the required fields (e.g Category, Venue, Gun Start etc.) to complete the process.
